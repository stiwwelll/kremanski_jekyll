It is a bundle of different things, that we put together in one bar – creative food, homemade ice-cream, special drinks and diverse cultural events. Book Kremanski for your own event!

You want to celebrate or invite friends or business partners – rent KREMANSKI!

Whether you want a special buffet, set dinner, live-music or DJ – we can talk about all your ideas. The whole place fits 40 to 90 people depending on the amount of tables. The small bar in the back fits up to 25 people standing. Reserving just one table is possible as well. In terms of financial conditions, we are open to make individual deals.

So, please feel free to talk to us about your personal idea and together we figure out something that fits!

Please note: Because of noise-sensitive neighbours we can´t be a real „club space“ until the morning hours.

From breakfast to lunch and from coffee break to evening drinks – our aim is simply to offer something good. Food, that is freshly made, creative and healthy. Beverages, that come from local providers. Like what we would offer our friends at home.

Please check out our menu!

Helpers to keep our standard high:

  • Espresso, roasted by BONANZA
  • Quality brewing by LA MARZOCCO
  • Daily fresh, fine and creative foods by JULIEN ROUVROY
  • Homemade Cakes by CAMILLA and friends
  • SUPERFOOD (CHIA grains, ACEROLA, ACAI) from Fine Fruits Company
  • Local quality beers from SCHOPPE BRÄU and BERLINER BERG
  • And many others!

As YOU, our clients, are the key to our efforts, please let us know your opinion on your KREMANSKI-experience. We are glad to make things even more satisfying for YOU!

Enjoy Kremanski at HOME or in your OFFICE:
If you want to enjoy a creative lunch once or every week together with your whole team in the office: no problem! Talk to us and we organize it "custom-made"!

You are also welcome to order BIG AMOUNTS (like 10-40 meals) – once or on a regular basis – please contact us and we will send an individual composition of meals to you!
